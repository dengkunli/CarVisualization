A visualization of global car characteristics between 1970 and 1982

The data that we visualize here is the Auto MPG Data Set which contains 398 automobile records from 1970 to 1982 of US, Japan and Europe. Each car is described by its model, region of production, year of production and 6 attributes: MPG (miles per gallon), number of cylinders, displacement, horsepower, weight, acceleration.
